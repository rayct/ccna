# GitHub Flavoured Markdown (GFM) Admonition Blocks

## Currently Supported types on GitHub:

- NOTE - General Information
>[!Note]
>Alway's take note of who's watching and listening

- TIP - Helpful advice or best practice
>[!Tip]
>Alway's check your seals for leaks. History is unkind to all those who play god.

- IMPORTANT - Critical information the reader should not miss
>[!Important]
>Important, never trust anyone!

- WARNING - Potential problems or dangers
>[!Warning]
>A warning to all those still in command. Never play god.

- CAUTION - Risk of damage, loss. or serious consequences
>[!Caution]
>Throw caution to the wind.
